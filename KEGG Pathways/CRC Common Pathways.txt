Sources:
1. Colorectal cancer – KEGG: hsa05210
	Description: This is a composite pathway in KEGG that integrates Wnt, p53, MAPK, and others specific to CRC.
    Reference:
        Kanehisa M, Furumichi M, Tanabe M, Sato Y, Morishima K. KEGG: integrating viruses and cellular organisms. Nucleic Acids Res. 2021;49(D1):D545–D551. doi:10.1093/nar/gkaa970

2. Wnt Signaling Pathway – KEGG: hsa04310
    Description: Central to CRC initiation. APC mutations lead to β-catenin accumulation.
    Reference:
        Polakis P. Wnt signaling in cancer. Cell. 2012;149(6):1192–205.doi:10.1016/j.cell.2012.05.012
        Segditsas S, Tomlinson I. Colorectal cancer and genetic alterations in the Wnt pathway. Oncogene. 2006;25(57):7531–7.
        
3. PI3K-Akt Signaling Pathway – KEGG: hsa04151
    Description: Regulates growth and survival; often altered via PIK3CA mutation in CRC.
    Reference:
        Frattini M, et al. PIK3CA gene mutations in colorectal cancer. J Clin Oncol. 2005;23(24):5705–12.
        Liu Y, et al. PI3K/Akt pathway in colorectal cancer: basic mechanisms and clinical implications. Genes & Diseases. 2020;7(3):297–302.
        
4. MAPK Signaling Pathway – KEGG: hsa04010
    Description: Activated downstream of EGFR/KRAS → promotes proliferation.
    Reference:
        McCubrey JA, et al. Roles of the Raf/MEK/ERK pathway in cell growth, malignant transformation and drug resistance. Biochim Biophys Acta. 2007;1773(8):1263–84.
        
5. TGF-beta Signaling Pathway – KEGG: hsa04350
    Description: Tumor suppressor in early CRC, tumor promoter in late stages (metastasis, EMT).
    Reference:
        Massagué J. TGFβ signalling in context. Nat Rev Mol Cell Biol. 2012;13(10):616–30.
        Grady WM, et al. TGF-beta signaling and colon cancer progression. Curr Colorectal Cancer Rep. 2005;1(2):62–66.
        
6. p53 Signaling Pathway – KEGG: hsa04115
    Description: p53 mutations are present in ~60% of CRC tumors.
    Reference:
        Baker SJ, et al. p53 mutations occur in a wide spectrum of human tumors. Science. 1990;249(4972):912–5.
        Fearon ER, Vogelstein B. A genetic model for colorectal tumorigenesis. Cell. 1990;61(5):759–67.

7. Hedgehog Signaling Pathway – KEGG: hsa04340
    Description: Less common, but linked to CRC stemness and chemoresistance.
    Reference:
        Varnat F, et al. Human colon cancer epithelial cells express active Hh signaling pathway components. PLoS One. 2009;4(3):e6159.
        Bian Y, et al. Activation of the hedgehog pathway in human colon cancer cells: regulation of proliferation and apoptosis by GLI1. Int J Oncol. 2007;30(6):139–47.

8. Apoptosis – KEGG: hsa04210
    Description: Inhibition of apoptosis (e.g., via BCL2) is key in CRC survival.
    Reference:
        Igney FH, Krammer PH. Death and anti-death: tumour resistance to apoptosis. Nat Rev Cancer. 2002;2(4):277–88.

9. Cell Cycle – KEGG: hsa04110
    Description: CRC cells often show deregulation of Cyclins, CDKs (e.g., CCND1 overexpression).
    Reference:
        Malumbres M, Barbacid M. Cell cycle, CDKs and cancer: a changing paradigm. Nat Rev Cancer. 2009;9(3):153–66.
